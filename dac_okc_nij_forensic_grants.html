Funding sources

Paul Coverdell Forensic Science Improvement Grants Program — annual NIJ grants to the Oklahoma District Attorneys Council as the designated state administering agency. Source for all DAC awards on this chart.

NIJ DNA Backlog Reduction / DNA Capacity Enhancement Program — annual formula and competitive grants administered by the National Institute of Justice through fiscal year 2015. Source for the City of Oklahoma City pre-2016 awards.

BJA DNA Capacity Enhancement for Backlog Reduction (CEBR) Program — successor program to the NIJ DNA Backlog Reduction grants beginning in fiscal year 2016, administered by the Bureau of Justice Assistance. Source for all post-2015 City of Oklahoma City awards on this chart.

Sources. Award listings retrieved from the National Institute of Justice (nij.ojp.gov) and Bureau of Justice Assistance (bja.ojp.gov) award databases, including individual award detail pages on those domains.
